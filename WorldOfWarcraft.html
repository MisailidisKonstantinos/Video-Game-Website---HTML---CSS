World of Warcraft

The creation of a new world

World of Warcraft was developep by Blizzard Entertainment, previously known for their Warcraft and Starcraft RTS series, and was released at November 23 2004. Instantly the entire world fell in love with this new Massively Multiplayer Online Role-playing Game (MMO RPG) and the huge amount of available quests, different and unique zones and the variety of Races and Classes that were available and divided between the two Faction, the Alliance and the Horde.
The game expended its own world even more with the expansions that were released and added more classes, races and even continents filled with new zones. The released expansions are provided in the list bellow:

  • The Burning Crusade (2007)
  • Wrath of the Lich King (2008)
  • Cataclysm (2010)
  • Mists of Pandaria (2012)
  • Warlords of Draenor (2014)
  • Legion (2016)
  • Battle for Azeroth (2018)
  • Shadowlands (2020)

In addition to all of the different expansions, Blizzard decided to release once again their original game and so World of Warcraft Classic was released (2019) and in June 1st 2021 the release of The Burning Crusade Classic is scheduled. These releases of the oldest stages of the game happened to satisfy the need for player to experience the old days that made them fans of the world of Azeroth.
